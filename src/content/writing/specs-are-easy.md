---
title: Specs are easy, shipping is the tax
date: 2026-05-30
summary: The document is the fun part. The tax is everything between it and a URL.
readtime: 5 min
filed:
  - process
---

A spec is a pleasure to write. It is all intent and no friction: every decision
clean, every tradeoff theoretical. Then you try to ship it and the tax comes
due: the edge cases, the dependency that won't build, the thing that looked
obvious on paper and is ambiguous in code.

The tax is the actual work. The spec just tells you what you're paying for.
