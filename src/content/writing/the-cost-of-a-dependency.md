---
title: The real cost of a dependency
date: 2026-04-05
summary: It isn't the install size. It's the surface you no longer control.
readtime: 5 min
filed:
  - process
---

A dependency is a promise someone else makes to you, and can break. The cost was
never the bytes. It is the API that shifts, the maintainer who walks, the CVE
you now have to track. Add them on purpose, not on reflex.
